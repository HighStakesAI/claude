Video Surveillance

Smarter Visibility. Stronger Security.

Don't have video surveillance? What are you missing? High-definition, IP-based systems built on advanced cameras, secure servers, and intelligent network design — deterring threats, documenting incidents, and covering high-traffic zones, restricted areas, and remote sites.

  • Remote access from mobile or desktop
  • Real-time monitoring with two-way “talk-down” communication
  • IPTV with multicasting for authorized viewers
  • Free site assessment with customized camera layouts

Full Capability

Every deployment starts with intelligent network design: high-definition IP cameras matched to your facility, secure servers to protect footage, and a layout engineered around how people and assets actually move through your site.

Operators monitor in real time and intervene through two-way talk-down communication before an incident escalates. IPTV with multicasting distributes live video to authorized viewers across your organization, while remote access keeps eyes on every high-traffic zone, restricted area, and remote site from any mobile device or desktop.

When something does happen, your system becomes your record — documenting incidents with the clarity investigations and insurers demand.

Verified intrusion changes the economics of alarm response. Motion, audio, and camera verification work together to confirm that a threat is real before anyone is dispatched — so responders arrive faster, with better information, and false alarms drop dramatically.

Infant Protection Systems

Keeping Newborns Safe From the Moment of Birth.

Soft, tamper-resistant tags with room-level tracking for hospitals, maternity wards, and birthing centers — built on the Accutech “Cuddles” platform.

  • Tags check in every 1.5 seconds
  • Staff alerts & lockdown typically within 15 seconds of signal loss
  • Operates independently of hospital networks, with battery backup
  • License-free software with no recurring costs

Full Capability

An infant can be removed from a room in roughly four seconds — and most abductions occur inside healthcare facilities. That is why automatic tag check-in matters: up to 90 seconds faster than competing systems.

Automated tag transfers follow mother and child through the facility, door-control integration locks down exits the moment a signal is lost, and the platform pairs with your existing CCTV. See the full spotlight below for how it works.
