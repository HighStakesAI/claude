Questions

Straight answers.

How much does it cost to paint a house interior?

Most single rooms run $450–$900. Full interiors typically land between $3,500 and $9,500 depending on square footage, ceiling height, and prep work. Every quote is written, itemized, and fixed — no surprises on the final invoice.

How long will my project take?

A standard room takes one day. Full interiors run 3–5 days, exteriors 4–7 depending on size and weather. You get a written schedule before we start, and we hit it — that's the on-time promise.

Do I need to move my furniture?

No. We move it, center it, and wrap it in clean plastic. Floors get rosin paper or drop cloths edge to edge. When we leave each day, your home is livable — not a job site.

What paint brands do you use?

Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore, matched to the surface: Duration and Emerald for exteriors, Emerald Urethane for cabinets, Cashmere and Regal Select for interiors. Prefer a specific product? We'll quote it.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — fully licensed, carrying $2M general liability and workers' comp on every crew member. Certificates of insurance are available before work begins, on request, no hesitation.

What does the 2-year warranty cover?

Peeling, blistering, flaking, and excessive fading on any surface we painted — labor and materials, in writing. If a wall fails inside two years, we come back and make it right at no cost.
