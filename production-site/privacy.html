Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how High Stakes ("High Stakes," "we," "us," or "our"), a business based in Pensacola, Florida, collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you visit histakesai.com (the "Site"), contact us, or use our services (collectively, the "Services"). By using the Site or Services, you agree to this Policy.

01Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information collected automatically when you use the Site.

Information you give us

  • Contact details — your name, email address, and phone number when you submit a form, request a quote, or message us.
  • Project information — details you share about your business or project.
  • Communications — the content of messages you send us by form, email, phone, or text.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data — pages viewed, links clicked, referring page, and time on site.
  • Device data — browser type, device type, and general (city/region) location derived from your IP address.
  • Cookies & analytics — see Section 6.

02How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide quotes, consultations, and customer support.
  • Deliver, maintain, and improve our Services.
  • Send you service-related and, where you have opted in, marketing communications by email, phone, or SMS/text message.
  • Measure and improve the performance of our Site and marketing.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud or misuse.

03SMS / Text Messaging

If you provide your phone number and check the consent box on our forms (or otherwise opt in), you agree to receive text messages from High Stakes related to your inquiry and our services — such as appointment reminders, account or project updates, customer care, and occasional offers.

  • Consent is not a condition of purchase. You are never required to agree to receive text messages in order to submit a form or use our Services.
  • Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.
  • Opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for help, or contact us at the details in Section 12.
Your mobile information is protected

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties, except for aggregators and providers of the Text Message services (for example, the messaging platform and carriers used to deliver the messages you requested).

04How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not buy or sell leads. We share information only in these limited situations:

  • Service providers — trusted vendors who perform services on our behalf (such as hosting, customer-relationship/marketing platforms, email and SMS delivery, and analytics), bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements — when required by law, subpoena, or to protect the rights, safety, and property of High Stakes, our clients, or others.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to this Policy.

All of the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties, excluding aggregators and providers of the Text Message services.

05Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When no longer needed, we delete or de-identify it.

06Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the Site, remember preferences, and understand how visitors use it. We use Google Analytics and Google Ads to measure traffic and the effectiveness of our advertising. You can control cookies through your browser settings and opt out of Google Analytics via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

07Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, to opt out of marketing communications, and to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information (we do not sell or share it for cross-context behavioral advertising). To exercise any right, contact us using Section 12 and we will respond as required by applicable law. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

08Children's Privacy

Our Site and Services are intended for businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

09Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10Third-Party Links

The Site may link to third-party websites or services we do not control. This Policy does not apply to those sites, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

11Changes to This Policy
