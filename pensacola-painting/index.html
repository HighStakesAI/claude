Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our Pensacola painting services.

Are you licensed and insured in Florida?

Yes, absolutely. We are fully licensed and insured painting contractors operating in the state of Florida. We carry comprehensive liability and workers' compensation insurance to protect you, your property, and our crew during every interior and exterior painting project.

What brand of paint do you use?

We exclusively use premium, high-durability paints from trusted brands like Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore. Using top-tier products ensures a flawless finish that resists fading, peeling, and can withstand the harsh Florida sun, humidity, and coastal weather conditions.

Do I need to move my own furniture before you paint?

No, our professional house painters handle the heavy lifting! We will carefully move your furniture to the center of the room, cover it entirely with clean drop cloths, and lay down protective floor coverings before a single drop of paint is opened.

How long does an exterior paint job typically take?

Most residential exterior painting projects in Pensacola and Gulf Breeze take between 3 to 5 days. However, this depends heavily on the size of the home, weather conditions, and the amount of prep work (like pressure washing, scraping, and siding repair) required to guarantee a perfect finish.
